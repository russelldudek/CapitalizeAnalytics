AI Engagement Canvas

Russell Dudek · Capitalize Analytics candidate campaign

Use this working surface to preserve one proof chain from decision through ownership.
Recommendations remain hypotheses until validated in discovery.

1 · Decision record

What business decision, workflow friction, consequence, and accountable owner define the problem?

2 · Leverage hypothesis

How should AI change economics, speed, quality, or risk? What evidence could disprove the bet?

3 · Authority boundary

What may the system do? What must a person decide, review, override, escalate, or stop?

4 · Evidence standard

Which representative cases, quality thresholds, business measures, and failure modes determine readiness?

5 · Handoff contract

What integrations, permissions, observability, support, rollback, and training obligations must be explicit?

6 · Ownership cadence

Who reviews performance, exceptions, user behavior, risk, and improvement after delivery?
