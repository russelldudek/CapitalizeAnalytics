AI Engagement Canvas

A practical discovery artifact for turning broad AI interest into a scopeable proof. Use in ideation, then carry the assumptions into estimate, build, evaluation, and retrospective.

1 · Business decision

What decision, workflow, delay, quality failure, or recurring exception are we trying to improve? Who owns it today?

2 · AI leverage

Should AI retrieve, predict, generate, classify, optimize, or act? What materially changes if it works?

3 · Human authority

What must a person approve, review, override, escalate, or stop? What consequence of error is unacceptable?

4 · Evidence & data

What representative cases, context, labels, history, policies, and edge conditions are available? What is missing?

5 · Proof design

What is the smallest credible demonstration? Which eval cases, failure modes, and business measures will decide whether to continue?

6 · Production handoff

What integrations, permissions, observability, rollback, support, documentation, training, and owner cadence are required?
