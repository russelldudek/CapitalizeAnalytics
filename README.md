## Campaign thesis

**Make AI credible in the room—and useful in the workflow.**

The campaign argues that strategy and delivery should form one continuous proof chain. Its operating artifact is the AI Proof Ledger: a persistent record of the decision, leverage hypothesis, authority boundary, evidence standard, handoff contract, and ownership cadence.

## Brand fidelity

The company-recognition layer uses:

- the locally committed, unmodified official Capitalize primary wordmark, white alternate wordmark, and favicon;
- public Elementor color tokens including `#012571`, `#0E41B8`, `#0C62FB`, `#3A3A3A`, `#EBEBEB`, and white;
- source-sampled official-logo blue and charcoal;
- Lora for headings and Open Sans for body copy, based on the official site's declared public typography;
- an explicit `Candidate vision for AI Senior Consultant` / `by Russell Dudek` / independent-candidate relationship above the fold.

The candidate-owned layer remains the AI Proof Ledger, its continuity motion, scenario lab, evidence architecture, objection strategy, and consulting thesis.

Official homepage photography was reviewed but intentionally not used because it did not materially improve the role argument and could create ambiguity about candidate-owned work or relationships. The campaign instead uses documented company geometry, rails, rules, density, color, and typography cues.

## Evidence integrity

Candidate claims are restricted to verified RoleForge evidence. Company-specific recommendations are labeled or treated as hypotheses unless supported by public sources. The campaign does not claim production ownership of every platform named in the posting.

## Verification

The campaign is generated, rendered, and audited through repository workflows that verify:

- the complete `main` manifest;
- official identity and brand tokens;
- desktop, laptop, tablet, mobile, and reduced-motion renders;
- keyboard interaction and no horizontal overflow;
- exact résumé and cover-letter page counts;
- every other PDF page count;
- rasterized PDF appearance, embedded brand images, and brand-color rendering;
- live GitHub Pages routes, local brand assets, reciprocal document links, and exact PDF hashes.

The final audited `main` head is recorded in the RoleForge portfolio index and final handoff after the last audit commit.
