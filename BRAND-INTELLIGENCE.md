## Compact record

**Official logo source:** Capitalize Analytics official website header/footer. The campaign does not embed the official logo because a stable downloadable brand-kit asset and explicit public reuse terms were not identified during this audit.

**Palette:** Employer-aware plum/magenta family, deep charcoal, white/light neutral fields, and bright lime used as a candidate-system accent. The exact candidate palette is original and is not presented as an official brand palette.

**Typography character:** Bold geometric sans-serif headlines, compact uppercase utility labels, and clear professional body copy. No official font claim is made.

**Photography/illustration character:** The official site combines service-led editorial content, technology-partner marks, and human/business imagery. The candidate campaign uses an original diagrammatic continuity system rather than hotlinked photography or partner logos.

**Geometry and interface motifs:** High-contrast bands, clear content rails, compact utility labels, strong modular sections, and confident service-navigation density.

**Motion character:** The official digital presence is service-led and structured rather than playful. The campaign uses calm, precise sequencing and state changes instead of decorative parallax or spectacle.

**Overall vibe:** Practical, business-first, modern, technically credible, and oriented toward reducing uncertainty.

**Approved or supportable assets:** Official public source links and company/role names used for attribution and research. No third-party logo aggregator assets are used.

**Original visuals created:** `assets/proof-ledger-mark.svg`, animated AI Proof Ledger hero, tension axis, continuity ledger sheet, scenario proof lab, evidence receipts, and score-line system.

**Recommended use by artifact:**

- Candidate site: stronger employer-aware plum/charcoal grammar and original lime continuity accent.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained plum rules and lime callout bars only.
- Interview brief and 90-day plan: dark cover, plum continuity rules, and light-neutral report pages.
- PDFs: identical print-safe visual system with no official-company impersonation.

## Usage constraints

- Preserve the distinction between candidate campaign and official company publication.
- Do not imply endorsement.
- Do not claim the candidate palette or typography is official.
- Do not hotlink external images or logos.
- Keep employer cues subtle in application documents.
