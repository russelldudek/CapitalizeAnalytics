Capitalize Analytics Hiring Team,

The interesting problem in this role is not whether a consultant can talk about AI. It is whether one person can help an executive team identify the right leverage point, scope the work honestly, build enough of the solution to earn technical trust, and carry the result through evaluation, handoff, and adoption. Your posting is unusually direct about wanting both sides of that work. That is precisely why I am interested.

My operating thesis is simple: strategy and delivery should form one continuous proof chain.

At Vape-Jet, I lead AI-first operating transformation across a connected system of production, quality, support, customer success, engineering issue flow, ERP/Odoo, Jira, HubSpot, Slack, telemetry, and AI-assisted workflows. The work has reinforced a lesson I learned earlier at Amazon and Compunetics: technology creates value only when the operating system around it makes decisions, ownership, escalation, and learning visible.

Through DudeWorth, I have developed AI-readiness and opportunity frameworks that examine value, feasibility, data readiness, governance, adoption burden, reuse, and time-to-value. I also design agentic workflow patterns that combine retrieval, structured context, human judgment, escalation, decision records, evaluation, and governance rails. I would bring that business-first framing into ideation and scoping while staying close enough to the build to make the proof credible.

I am candid about the obvious question. I do not claim production ownership of every platform named in the posting. My verified toolkit includes Python, SQL, TensorFlow, LLM and RAG patterns, agentic workflows, and human-in-the-loop design. My differentiator is technical fluency combined with direct accountability for the operations, people, controls, and consequences that AI must improve. A practical case or pair-build exercise would be the fairest way to test that claim.

Capitalize’s defined ideation entry point and 45-day AI-to-ROI offer signal a practice moving from market curiosity toward repeatable production credibility. I would be excited to help make that bridge easier to sell, easier to deliver, and easier for clients to trust and own.

Carpe diem,
